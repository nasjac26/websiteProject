Plan a Boardgame Night!

Suggestions for many occasions and tastes

Isn't it a drag when you are craving a board game but can't get a large group together? 7 Wonders Duel takes the ideas from the parent game 7 Wonders and adapts it for two players.

  • Lead a civilization.
  • Will you invest in trade, the sciences, art, or your military?
  • Find balance in not to let your oppenent get to far ahead in any of these things.
