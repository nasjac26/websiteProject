BOARD GAME NIGHT FOR TWO?

7 Wonders Duel

Isn't it a drag when you are craving a board game but can't get a large group together? 7 Wonders Duel takes the ideas from the parent game 7 Wonders and adapts it for two players.

  • Lead a civilization.
  • Will you invest in trade, the sciences, art, or your military?
  • Take care not to let your oppenent get to far ahead in any of these things.
  • Easy to read iconography.
  • Low investment cost at around $30.

LOVE BRAWLING?

Kemet

This one goes out to all the people who grew up loving Risk. Kemet is a combat heavy board game centered around area control and creating a unique army every game.

  • Become a god of ancient Egypt.
  • Create and design your faction by investing in one of a kind tiles that enhance your abilites.
  • Will you excel in attacking, defending, or your economy by building your religion?
  • Game rewards being aggressive instead of waiting for other people to fight.
  • Beautiful minatures
  • While the original is out of print, you can preorder the reprint Kemet: Blood and Sand

WANT SOMETHING COOPERATIVE?

Pandemic

  • Work together with other players to stop 4 deadly diseases.
  • Each player gets a unique ability to contribute to success of the group.
  • Easy to learn, Quick to teach
  • You can find this one almost anywhere including Target, Walmart and Barnes and Nobles
